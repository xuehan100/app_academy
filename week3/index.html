Salted Foam Cold Brew

  • ingredients
  • directions

Recipe Description

an easy & natural recipe for homemade starbucks salted cream cold foam cold brew with only 4 ingredients! cold brew coffee, lightly sweetened with maple syrup, topped with a creamy salty-sweet cold foam, this salted cream cold foam cold brew coffee is the perfect salty-sweet coffee drink!

wikipedia

Ingredients

  • cold brew coffee
  • milk. preferably skim for optimal froth-age(or spring for half & half, or even heavy cream for optimal treat-age)
  • salt
  • maple syrup

Directions

  1. brew your cold brew.
    my ultimate lazy girl cold brew hack is making a batch of cold brew in a french press. you add one cup of fresh coarsely ground coffee to a french press, then you fill it on up with water (about 4 cups, but seriously, who has time to measure these things?). let it sit overnight, then the next morning you’ve got yourself perfect cold brew that took about 2 minutes of active prep time to make.
  2. the salted cream cold foam.
    pop some skim milk(!) in a milk frother, with a little bit of salt & a little drizzle of maple for optimal salty-sweetness. i could seriously eat this stuff with my hands – it is SO freakin’ good!
  3. you put the whole thing together.
    i add a little salt to my coffee, because my obsession for salty coffee is a little out of control now. & if i’m feeling fancy? a little extra maple syrup.
